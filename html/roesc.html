Romania in Eurovision Song Contest

Romania has participated in the Eurovision Song Contest 21 times since its debut in 1994, and has placed in the top ten six times.
In 1993 Romania attempted to debut in the contest, but came last in the pre-qualifying round. After successfully joining the following year, poor placements followed until 2002.
This changed with the introduction of semi-finals to the contest in 2004, after which Romania reached the final 13 times.

Best results:

  • Luminița Anghel and Sistem"Let Me Try"(2005)
  • Mihai Trăistariu "Tornerò"(2006)
  • Paula Seling and Ovi "Playing with Fire"(2010)
